DREAM LAND

The Dream Land is reachable from Chapter 2 and onwards.
In Chapter # the Paths are available, they are locked off before that.

The Hub

The Hub is the middle section in Dream Land and can be considered pretty safe. The Hub section contains:

The Bedroom

Where KAR wakes up in his dream and the place to preferably fall asleep and then wake up in the apartment. Falling asleep/waking up outside the bed has consequences.

The Dream Ministry

A place of specific knowledge. The person behind the desk knows everything about KAR's daily life. He keeps track of everything. Which is awkward but also useful.

The Shop

The Garden

A place for KAR to fetch soil and plant dream seeds.

The Puddle

At first glance, nothing but a deep puddle, but also Mrs. Octo and Mr. Octo's home.

The Gravyard

???

The Paths

The six different paths leading from the hub. Each path contains something that prevents KAR from reaching the room at the end of the path. Once there, KAR will have to solve a problem to get the reward (corridor apartment key).
The paths are:

The Swan Path

This path leads through reed beds and lots of dead and barely living dream fish and ends at the lake with the Swan.

The Tiger Path

This path leads through spruce forests to end in the Tiger's clearing

The Fox Path

This path leads over the meadows to end in the fox pot

The Owl Path

This path leads up in the big tree and ends in the owl's nest.

ALTERNATIVELY:
The path to the owls tree is a maze made of bushes. After the Owl has awoken, the bushes turn thorny and dangerous.

Owl's Nest

The Yoni Path

This path differs from the others.

Yonis

Hidden Paths
