MECHANICS

Loop

The main game loop of the game is a day for KAR.
KAR wakes up in the apartment, uses up their action points and falls asleep and goes to the Dream Land.
When KAR goes back to bed, or dies, in the Dream Land they wake up on the following day.
Every new day triggers one or more events that KAR needs to deal with.
During the second and third chapters of the game, TOB also asks KAR to perform chores for him. If KAR refuses or forgets, TOB punishes him.
After enough chores, The Egg cracks open and spawns a new KAR, TOB brings the old KAR down into the sewers and the game starts over.
Every new KAR is slightly worse off than the last, making the game a little bit harder with every loop.
If KAR dies, the game starts over with a fresh KAR.

"Real end"

If KAR manages to get down into the Sewers without TOB, he'll break the cycle and beat the game.
To get into the Sewers, KAR needs to obtain 4 keys from the Dream Animals.
The keys unlock doors in the Corridor where KAR finds objects that are used to unblock the rooms in the Yard.

Events

there are 3 types of events, TOB, Triggered and Visitor.

TOB:
Every morning when KAR wakes up, a random TOB event is triggered.

Triggered:
Triggered events are most often triggered when KAR wakes up in the morning, or at a set time after KAR does some certain action.

Visitor:
Every few days, a random visitor event gets triggered.

Action Points

KAR has a limited amount of Action Points (AP).
When KAR runs out of AP, KAR falls asleep and goes to Dream Land.
Sleeping restores some AP.
Doing chores for TOB may reward candies that restore AP.

Chapters

The game as a whole is divided up into 3 chapters.
See CHAPTERS for more detail.

Feeding The Baby

TOB is hungry every day when KAR wakes up.
TOB gives KAR a baby bottle, to be filled up and handed back.
TOB accepts his sewer food in any container (an empty can, wine bottle, or a rag will do), but will only reward AP-candy if he gets fed properly with a closed Baby Bottle.
Sewer Food is made by mixing a Sewer Ball in a blender.
Sewer Balls can be fished up out of the toilet, new ones spawn at set intervals.
On some days TOB has got cravings for something extra. KAR simply needs to add that extra ingredient to the blender before mixing.

recipe ideas:
sewer ball + meat = Meaty Sewer Stew
sewer ball + fruit = Fermented Sewer Fruit
sewer ball + soil = Earthen Sewer Mash
sewer ball + something rotten = Putrid Sewer Slop
sewer ball + alcohol = Distilled Sewer Brew
sewer ball + small animal = Protein Sewer Ration
sewer ball + bones = Calcified Sewer Stock
sewer ball + pills = Medicinal Sewer Paste
sewer ball + dream goat milk = Somnolent Sewer Shake
