TOB_EVENTS

When KAR wakes up every morning, TOB has done something in the apartment.

The Smoothie

Recurring event. TOB demands food (blended in the blender) consisting of yummy sewage balls (from toilet) or regular food impregnated with sewage water every day. If he doesn't have his daily ration of sewage smoothie, he becomes ill-tempered and uncooperative.

The Exception

A satisfied TOB is rare. A satisfied TOB leaves KAR mostly alone. But it happens, if TOB is exceptionally satisfied, that he does nice things in the apartment while KAR asleep.

  • watering flowers
  • picking up trash / cleaning
  • stocking up on food
  • decorating
  • make breakfast for KAR
  • leaving gifts

The Nails

KAR can't open some doors in the apartment. They are nailed shut.
KAR needs to find a tool to pull out the nails in order to open the nailed doors.

The Starve

All the food is eaten / ruined / pissed on / flushed down / thrown out.
KAR needs to find new food.

The Scissors

A malicious TOB can go wild with the scissors while KARs asleep

  • plants
  • clothes
  • books
  • posters
  • pillows
  • tablecloths
  • rugs
  • pets

The Shards

Someone has broken all the glass bottles in the apartment.
KAR needs to clean up before someone gets hurt.

The Kitten Massacre

KAR wakes up to discover a room filled with dead kittens.
A malicious TOB combined with possession of kittens is an unfortunate combination.

The Dead Pet

KAR wakes up to find his pet killed.
A malicious TOB combined with possession of a beloved pet is an unfortunate combination.

The Awakening

Malicious TOB wakes KAR up in the middle of the night in various unpleasant ways:

  • suffocates him
  • pees on him
  • hits him with a hammer
  • attempts to saw off limbs
  • applies the AP suction machine
  • etc.
    Disturbs KAR's sleep and thus his presence in the dream world.

The Toothless

Requires a really ill-tempered TOB. KAR is awakened by TOB hitting him in the face with a hammer, resulting in him losing all his teeth. KAR can now only eat liquid food unless he figures out a way to put his teeth back in.

The Whining

KAR can't sleep because something wonÂ´t stop whining in the yard. If KAR looks out, he might see a strange creature (from the sewers, calling out to TOB?) staring up at him and whining. Unless KAR finds a way to make it go away, there will be more creatures the next night (the same thing if he tries to ignore it).

The Messages

KAR's most intimate secrets have been written across the walls of the apartment with what appears to be feces. KAR needs to get the mess removed before anyone (worst case, the property manager) sees it, and to get peace of mind.

The Murals

TOB likes to draw strange symbols and illustrations on the apartment walls. This makes KAR really uncomfortable. KAR needs to get the mess removed before anyone (worst case, the property manager) sees it, and to get peace of mind.

The Campsite

Someone has built a rudimentary fireplace out of bricks, broken up floorboards, and butchered a wooden chair and some kind of animal somewhere in the apartment. KAR needs to get the mess removed before anyone (worst case, the property manager) sees it, and to get peace of mind.

The Poisoning

KAR suspects that someone has tampered with the food. Does KAR dare to eat it?
KAR needs to find new food.

The Intestines

Piles of entrails makes life miserable for KAR.
KAR needs to get the mess removed before anyone (worst case, the property manager) sees it, and to get peace of mind.

The Stomach Pains

KAR wakes up with horrible stomach pains (TOB has offered KAR something strange to eat when KAR is starving?). Something wants to come out. KAR urgently needs to self-medicate with hard liquor to kill what is trying to grow big and powerful in his stomach.

The Lost Brain

KAR wakes up with his skull sawed open. His brain is missing and the scalp is on the floor next to the bed. KAR cannot interact and can only drag himself around the apartment like a drooling zombie. Get back into bed and guide the brain back to KAR's head from its hiding place.

The Eye

An eye sits on the floor, staring at KAR.
It grows over a few days before turning into a black hole that sucks nearby objects into it.

The Dead

TOB has left a handfull of small dead animals strewn around the apartment.

The Flesh Eating Plant

The Plant is present and KAR hasn't taken care of it (watered it).
Eats small animals such as dogs, cats, birds if they get too close.
If not fed, eventually breaks free and starts walking around using it's roots as legs.

The Little Body

KAR finds one of the neighbor's children dead in the apartment.

The Nothing

KAR becomes invisible.
KAR can't inspect themself.

The Old Shadow

KAR's shadow is acting strange.
todo: event needs more fleshing out.

The Mirror

KAR's reflection is acting strange.
Breaking the mirror ends the event.

The Other Me

KAR wakes up to a clone of himself in the apartment.
The clone follows KAR around.
It asks annoying questions.
"Is this it?"
"Is this who I am?"
"Am I you?"
"Are you me?"
todo: how do you get rid of the clone in a responsible way?

The Vent

Something is making noise from the ventilations.
Opening the vent to figure out what it is ends the event.
The more days it is present and not taken care of, the larger the thing hiding inside.

The Hole

A small hole appears in a wall or the floor.
KAR can peak inside to find something.

The Half Pet

KAR finds one half of his beloved pet. Where is the other part?

The Heavy Day

Everything is heavier than usual.
Picking up anything costs AP.
Getting out of bed, off the couch etc costs 2x AP.

The Mystery Box

A locked box is found in the apartment.
KAR needs a key to open it.

The Touch of Death

Anything KAR touches today immediately starts decaying/molding and spawns flies.

The Skinlessness

KAR has no skin and bleeds all the time.
Very sensitive to touch, obviously.
