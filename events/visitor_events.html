VISITOR_EVENTS

A neighbor or something visits KAR.

The Bird

A bird flies in through an open or broken window.
It might bring something in its claws.
They lose feathers and empty their bowels on the floor if they are startled.

The Fish

A fish peaks out of the toilet.
It might bring something from the sewers.
KAR can talk to the fish.

The Pet Sitter

Neighbor gives KAR their pet, to take care of for a few days.
Neighbor willll come back to pick it up, and gives a reward if KAR manages to give the dog back in one piece.

The Baby Sitter

ANN hands over her kid (kid may be familiar from previous events). This is no big issue if KAR is in balance and prepared with vhs movies, popcorn and spiked soda. If not, it can be all the more problematic.

The Lost Property

A random neighbor comes by and asks for an item they are missing for various reasons.

The Box of Meow

KAR comes across a box filled with dead or living kittens.

The Bag of Meow

KAR comes across a bag with dead or living kittens.

The Kid

KID rings the door and slips into KARs apartment.
Screams and tosses things around if KAR gets close to them.
KAR needs to pick up KID and toss em out in the hallway again.

The Mute Kid

A strange kid comes to visit. The kid follows KAR and stares at him without saying anything. This makes KAR uncomfortable.

The Wrapped Kid

A kid with duct tape wrapped around his head bangs on door. It runs into the apartment if KAR opens. If KAR removes the tape, the child starts screaming hysterically and refuses to stop, or bounces around of joy and gives KAR a bag of mixed AP candy (small ones). If KAR does not help the child, the child collapses on the floor and suffocates.

The Sausage Kid

A hurt child rings the doorbell. He asks for help and claims to have been attacked by a stray dog in the hallway. If KAR helps the child (bandages it), KAR receives a special sausage that the child has picked up in the hallway. The sausage may come in handy in the future.

The Lost Kid

A kid rings the doorbell and says that he has been thrown out by his mean parents because they claim he ate an entire package of cornflakes. If KAR has a package of cornflakes and gives it to the kid, the kid will laugh and run away. If KAR does not give a package, the child get angry, pee on the floor and threaten that his mother will come and stab KAR in both eyes with a knife. And maybe she will.

The Bloated Cat

KAR for a visit from a bloated cat. It seems pregnant.
It can give birth to between 5 and 15 kittens. Kittens are cute and precious. However, some cats are bloated for reasons other than pregnancy. Keep or get rid of?

The Bloated Dog

Dogs eat some really disgusting things that can cause their intestines to produce corrosive feces and unhealthy gases. But dogs can get pregnant too. Puppies are cute and precious.

The Pom Invasion

SIV's dogs can run around in the hallway and create a havoc and are happy to run into KAR's apartment if he opens the door at the wrong moment. They bark, bite and poop constantly.
Get them out, but be careful: If anything happens to these little shitheads, Siv and Man will make KAR's life even more miserable.

The Exploding Poms

Pompoms have very sensitive stomachs. If they happen to eat anything other than dog food, they will explode. Creates a sloppy mess and angry owners.

The Pig Head

Someone has placed a severed pig's head outside KAR's front door.

The Note

Neighbor slips a note in KARs mailbox. Often containing nonsense or threats, but sometimes also useful information.

The Hassler

Recurring event, where SEB comes by. SEB is either chaotic (high/drunk) or calm and quite likeable (somewhat sober). If he is chaotic, he behaves irrationally: picks up things, drops things (a plastic bag with random things he took from other neighbors), talks incoherently, has mood swings, leaves the front door open, etc.

The risk, however, is that if SEB comes back before he sobers up to pick up his things. He will not be happy if they are gone.

When sober, he is more of a businessman who likes to barter with KAR. For example, various useful pills for random saleable items.

SEB won't come visit KAR sober if he's mad at him.

The Troubadour

Recurring event. Kalkan is a lonely man who craves attention. He likes to knock on KAR's door in the hope of being invited for a drink or more and get the opportunity perform his his own composed song to an audience.

He is very interested in hearing what KAR thinks of the song and always thinks KAR hates it, no matter what KAR says to him.
Kalkan tends to get inappropriately emotional, wants to be hugged and refuses to leave (sieges the couch).

Kalkan has a CD of his music that he wants to sell to KAR.

Kalkan is talking about a video recording that contains evidence of him performing at a local pub thirteen years ago. But he can't find it anywhere.

Kalkan claims that little furry demons gnaw on the strings of his guitar when he sleeps. Some nights even on his vocal cords.
A big reason why he is afraid to sleep alone.

How does KAR get rid of Kalkan?

The Visions of Siv

Recurring event. She has recurring visions that tell her about KAR's fate, which she of course needs to tell KAR about. SIV wants special dream stones, of which she makes spiritually strengthening necklaces and such.

SIV gets upset if KAR doesn't give here new dream stones when she asks for them.
She can try to give KAR cursed tokens or send MAN to correct KAR's behavior.

The Movie Freak

If KAR manages to lure Mr Octo to the dream world by having the Octoman (1971) movie on VHS in his possession:
Mr Octo besieges KAR's couch and demands that the same movie be played 24/7 for three days. If KAR fails to rewind the movie in time, Mr Octo gets angry and makes the living room a no-go zone for KAR. Mr. Octo likes to eat pizza and pets.

The Manly Man

If KAR upsets SIV, MAN will come to visit on her behalf. He will calmly explain what KAR needs to do to make things right. If KAR doesn't do as he says, MAN will be a pain to deal with in the future.

MAN doesn't like KAR giving SIV stones. She doesn't talk about anything else and is acting weirder than usual. The stones are obviously cursed, according to MAN.

MAN can also appear KAR fails to evict The Pompoms from the apartment in a gentle manner. In this situation, he is the opposite of calm.

MAN is capable of breaking down KAR's front door if really enraged.

The Dissolving Banker

JON is convinced that there is another half-naked person running around the house, imitating him, behaving badly and spreading lies. Which is not at all strange, according to Jon, as people who have not succeeded in life have a hard time dealing with the fact that he has it. They are jealous.

JON wants KAR to keep his eyes open and let him know if he encounters the impersonator.

When KAR encounters the "impersonator", who really looks just like a half-naked, fucked up JON (it is), he is happy to tell you what he thinks about the real JON, as well as share various secrets about him.
