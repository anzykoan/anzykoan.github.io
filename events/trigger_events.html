TRIGGER_EVENTS

Based on KARs actions, an event can trigger in the apartment.
They can trigger after a set time, or the next morning as KAR wakes up.
Can be thought of as "consequences" rather than events I guess.

The Coffee

Running out of coffee is a major problem, especially when it is required to keep special visitors happy. KAR needs to find ways to get hold of coffee package.

  • Neighbors' delivered grocery bags in the hallway
  • Barter with neighbor
  • Dropped food delivery bags
  • Buy from Shop Keep
  • Growing coffee beans in the dream garden?

The Toad

Too many flies in the apartment lures Toads out of the toilet.
The toad is very voracious and disgusting and is quickly growing problematically large unless KAR puts a stop to it.

The Hiding

If KAR falls asleep with the front door open (can be left open for various reasons), it can lead to one thing or another.
In this case, a creature (or kids) has snuck in and found a hiding place in the apartment.

  • spots: cabinets, bathtub, rug, sofa cushion, piles of trash and such
  • Something is hiding under the rug. Stomp on or investigate?
  • A moan is heard from a hiding place.
  • A gurgling sound is heard from a hiding place.
  • Cabinet door is ajar

The Burglary

A burglary has occurred and various items have been stolen.

The Throbbing

Knocks on the wall when KAR moves or interacts.
Someone behind the wall thinks KAR has been running around and slamming doors too much.
KAR needs to stay quiet for a longer period of time to silence the knocking.

The Colony

Too much waste/garbage in one room creates the conditions for a rat colony to make itself at home. There are more rats every day. KAR needs to stop this by removing the trash and wipe out the colony.

The Smell

KAR has left something stinky laying around. The smell affects the entire room / container.
Triggers the next morning.

The Gnawing Shrimp

The Shrimp can get stuck in a state where she can't stop barking. This might be triggered by too many visitors and noisy neighbors, noise from the yard, etc. If KAR doesn't manage to calm her down, she will bounce around and bark until she shits herself and collapses

The Shivering Shrimp

Sudden noises can cause The Shrimp to start shaking uncontrollably. KAR needs to calm her down or she will shake until she shits herself and collapses

The Crash

Requires upset neighbor. An object flies through the window in the middle of the night.

The Itch

KAR has had an allergic reaction after eating an apple (random thing / playthrough?) and is itching badly. KAR needs to find relief before he tears off his skin.

The Skinlessness

If, for example, KAR cannot stop scratching due to severe itching.
KAR has no skin and bleeds all the time.
Very sensitive to touch, obviously.
KAR needs to get some new skin.

The Rotten Limb

Untreated rat bites can cause blood poisoning that causes KAR's limbs to rot. KAR needs to act before the rot reaches the heart.

The Tummy Worm

KAR ate something bad and gets worms. KAR gets hungry faster and get less positive effects from eating.

The Draught

KAR has made the property manager unhappy.
All water is turned off in the apartment for the entire day.

The Blackout

KAR has made the property manager unhappy.
The power is turned off in the apartment for the entire day.

The Warm Day

KAR has made the property manager unhappy.
It's, like, really warm the entire day.
KAR sweats a lot. Stay hydrated, friend.

The Runs

KAR ate something bad and gets diarrhea.
KAR's stomach will increasingly terrorize KAR in the coming days if KAR does not find a way to cure it, or at least some way to gain control of the situation.

The Plant

KAR left a seed or fruit with seeds somewhere and now it's taken root and started growing from under the floorboards. If KAR has a crowbar, he can remove the floorboard and investigate the root of the plant that can be useful as an smoothie ingredient.

The Creak

A floorboard in the living room creaks when you step on it. If KAR has a crowbar, he can remove the floorboard and investigate. May or may not offer a reward.

The Plague

KAR touches a dead animal that's been laying around for too long and contracts a strange disease.
//todo: effects?

The Violence

KAR hears a fight outside in the hallway. If KAR goes out when it is quiet, he may find

  • pools of blood, teeth, hair
  • body parts
  • bag with items
  • weapons / tools
    If KAR goes out before it is quiet, KAR will also be attacked.

The Officer

Requires upset neighbor.
A police officer rings the doorbell and explains that they have received a report of concern and asks if everything is okay.

A police officer knocks on the door and explains that they have received a report of concern and asks if everything is okay.

A police officer bangs on the door and demands that KAR open. Immediately.

The Clog

KAR didn't flush the toilet, or left something in the sink.
And now it's clogged up.

Flushing the things down the toilet may be convenient, but may also clog it.

The Shit Shrooms

Interesting mushrooms can start growing in old turds if left laying around.
Ingredient for cleansing smoothie (to empty oneself of problematic content)?

The Wound Shrooms

An untreated wound might get a fungal infection. Not particularly healthy, but these kind of special mushrooms can be useful.

The Cough

KAR has been exposed to the cold and wakes up with a strange cough. KAR's lungs will terrorize KAR increasingly in the coming days if KAR doesn't find a way to cure it (unless he wants to meet his lungs in person). May annoy neighbors.
