CHARACTERS

KAR

The player KARacter. A diffuse personality. Humble, but lost and gullible. Doesn't feel good about performing morally questionable actions, but still does it out of naivety, fear of conflict. Trying to achieve good things by doing bad things.
Trying to accommodate his inner voice of an evil, manipulative, drowned kid, and at the same time maintain a functional everyday life

TOB

The Other Boy. Mostly stays in KARs closet during the days and gives KAR chores.

TOB is a figment of KAR's imagination. TOB claims to be KAR's childhood friend who supposedly fell and drowned and disappeared into a sewer sometime in the nineties. TOB also claims that KAR let this happen. But that he is no longer angry with KAR as they were "just kids" at the time of the incident. TOB understands, however, that this has led to KAR feeling like a very bad person. TOB wants to help KAR become a better person. To achieve this, KAR needs to do exactly what TOB asks.

  • TOB hates sunlight
  • TOB hates pets
  • TOB hates disobedience
  • TOB smells rotten
  • TOB is wet

SEB

Sebastian Sparre. Neighbor. Comes visiting sometimes.

  • Demanding
  • Unpredictable
  • Kleptomaniac
  • Generous (sometimes)
  • Drug addict
  • Animal lover

Asks for: pet related stuff, find things he claims to have lost (often pets and valuable things), borrow anything of value
Helps with: drops bags of "borrowed" stuff at KAR sometimes, brings gifts when he is "happy", a walking medicine cabinet

KALKAN

Karl-Kaj Algren. Former music school teacher. Now early retired, local troubadour (according to himself famous). A rather likeable neighbor, whose delusions of grandeur mixed with self-pity can get on one's nerves.

  • Attention-seeking
  • Self-pity
    Asks for: Booze, cigarettes, guitar strings, an audience
    Helps with: Calm things down with music

SIV

Siv Jakobsson is stuck in the spiritual world. Difficult to communicate with. Have strange wishes and ideas about most things. Love shiny stones, rat tails and bird claws etc. Siv gets offended easily.
Lives with MAN and owns three small dogs (pompoms).
Asks for: Weird objects to make totem necklaces which gives spiritual strength,
Helps with: spiritual some what unreliable strength

OLI

Oliver Brovic. Nervous and cryptic gentleman. Building something very advanced in his apartment.
Asking for: strange books, technical components, pills, coffee
Helps with: solving riddles

MAN

A little scared boy in the shape of a very large man. Living with Siv and her three pompoms. Haven't found any other asset in life than overreaction and excessive violence.

ANN

Ann Birgersson. Mother of the two uncontrollable kids ravaging the neighborhood. Nurse on sick leave.
Asks for: Her kids, cigarettes, sleeping pills, candy, kid-size cages
Helps with: some nursing care, medical equipment (medkit)

JON

Jon Berglund is a broker/banker who lives on the verge of the collapse. Hysterically nice person tries to maintain the appearance of normality, even if everything is already lost. He is seriously concerned that people think he is wierd and poor. This takes up all his time.
Becoming increasingly confused. He's a ticking bomb.

ABI

Ali Bilal. Heart-sick Syrian exile poet. Sympathetic. Ali is terrorized by neighbor (he wont tell who). Would like to stay in KAR's apartment and write The Poem. May have cardiac arrest by barking dogs and other sudden loud noises.
Asks for: beta blockers, animals to pet, cigarettes, coffee, a locked room (peace and quiet)
Helps with: Talking to ABI calms KAR, KAR needs the finished Poem for some reason
//todo: reason

JAN

Jan is a property manager. He is taciturn and very duty driven. If something needs to be done, it will be done. He has the main key and is happy to check an extra time if KAR's kitchen fan is working properly. Likes to be invited to coffee and preferably some snacks. Dislikes messy tenants. Be sure to be properly dressed when he comes to visit (anytime). Gets grumpy easily. Far-sighted.
Asks for: coffee, snacks and good behavior
Helps with: property problems (if not grumpy)

JAN JUNIOR

Daniel. The property manager's brain-damaged son. Claw-fingered and messy. Hurts pets and some times hugging them til death. Joins Jan to work sometimes. The frequency tends to correlate with the level of Jan's grumpiness.
Asks for: a spanking
Helps with: nothing at all

KIDS

Pranksters who buzz on KARs doorbell run away / run in and more.

SMOKER

During the course of the game, the smoker throws his apartment's contents out into the yard, blocking the way between rooms. The first time KAR encounters the smoker is in the Dream Land when he wants to set fire to the swan.
Asks for: a light / fire
Helps with: escalates the last stage of blockage (burning household pile) in the yard

BUILDER

The builder is a neighbor who during the game builds a wooden wall between the rooms in the yard. The builder has had a revelation about walls needing to be built wherever there are none. The first time KAR meets the Builder is on the Owl path in the Dream World, where his building ladders and wooden houses for the visitors of the owl.

CRYER

Cryers are schoolchildren who appear in the hallway outside KAR's apartment. They may have useful things in their backpack, but it's not always worth the risk of trying to investigate.

BUREAUCRAT

Keeps track of KAR's doings. A neighbor that KAR never meets in a person in an awake state. He informs KAR about important things through anonymous notes in the letter box. In the Dream World, he manifests himself as the bureaucrat. A helpful, but also zealous gentleman.

DELIVERY / SHOP KEEP

Food delivery with three personalities. Moves around in the corridor, looking for the right door, often at various stages of confused state (depending on active personality).


The food delivery is also a business owner in The Dream Land. It is not always easy to do business with three psychotic traders at the same time.

FOX

(Dream Animal)
The dream fox seems to be a trigger happy righteous man. A kind of sheriff of the dream world? He hates big and little fuckers for understandable reasons and needs KAR's help.

LIL FOX

The little fox is kidnapped by Big Fucker because of his tourette syndrome.

OWL

(Dream Animal)
Not all owls smart. Or even make sense. No matter what KAR needs to do what the this weirdo is asking.

TIGR

(Dream Animal)
Even tigers can become desperate for friendship. Captures and cages the animals of the forest to ensure that they remain with her. Best friends forever!

SWAN
